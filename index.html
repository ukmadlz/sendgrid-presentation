What is SendGrid?

All things e-mail

SendGrid is a service that covers all things e-mail:

  • Transactional E-mail
  • Newsletter/Marketing E-mail
  • Also handle parsing of inbound e-mail

Transactional e-mail is those that a system will send on request or during a certain action. Example's include
